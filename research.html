Research Interests:

I pursue both empirical and modeling approach to study (1) Sharing Economy business model and demand cannibalization (2) Closed-Loop Supply Chain to save resource and improve profitability (3) Remanufacturing products and second-hand market. I pursue my research to be applicable to the real-world, and sympathetic to societal preferences.
